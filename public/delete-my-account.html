Delete My Account

User Self-Service Account Deletion

⚠️ PERMANENT ACTION WARNING

  • ✗ This action is IRREVERSIBLE
  • ✗ All your data will be PERMANENTLY DELETED
  • ✗ All your tokens will be REVOKED
  • ✗ You cannot recover your account after deletion

📋 How It Works:

  1. 1. Enter your email and password
  2. 2. Check the confirmation checkbox
  3. 3. Click "DELETE MY ACCOUNT PERMANENTLY"
  4. 4. Your credentials will be verified
  5. 5. Your account will be permanently deleted
🔧 Technical Details

Method: GET (with URL parameters)

Endpoint: /delete-my-account?email={email}&password={password}

Authentication: Email + Password verification

Security: Password hash comparison

Protection: Admin accounts cannot be deleted

⚠️ Note: Credentials are sent in URL (visible in browser history)

💡 API Alternative: Developers can use the API endpoint: GET /api/delete-account-via-credentials?email={email}&password={password}
